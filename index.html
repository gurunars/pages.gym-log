Gym-Log

The app starts with a screen that corresponds to the current day and a floating "plus" button that leads to the entry creation menu.

Once the plus button is clicked - a menu is opened. Multiple types of entries can be created by clicking the respective icons.

A food entry contains a title and a mass.

A timestamp is just an entry that is supposed to denote a significant time. Like the time when you came to gym.

A comment is a brief piece of information that could clarify a training routine.

A stretching exercise has a title and a duration.

A cardio exercise has a title, a distance and a duration.

A drink has a title and volume.

A strength exercise has a title, a number of reps, weight and a duration of the set.

A supplement has a title and a mass.

A break has just a duration.

A session can be saved as a template for further use as a blueprint for a new day.

It is possible to navigate between different days using a calendar widget.

Templates can be used to populate a new day.

One of the existing templates has to be selected.

Once the template is selected - a session is populated with a bunch of blueprint entries.

The entries are supposed to be edited by tapping them. Once the entry is edited it stops being a template.

It is possible to toggle a measurement system and backup data using a settings screen.

Changing a measurement system does not perform any recalculations of the original values.

Entries can be manipulated by long clicking one of them.

Entries can be moved up and down.

Entries can be removed.
